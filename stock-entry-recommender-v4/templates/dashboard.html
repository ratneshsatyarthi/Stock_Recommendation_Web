Signal Rules

1. Global scoring

Each symbol gets a composite score from delivery, trade, price change, repeat days and trend:

  • Delivery vs Trade ratio: ratio = Delv x / (Trade x + 0.1)
  • Delivery spike bonus when Delv x > 1.3 × avg Delv x, else a smaller bonus if Delv x > avg Delv x
  • Trade spike bonus when Trade x > 1.5 × avg Trade x
  • Price change bonus / penalty from Chg% (strongly positive, mildly positive, or < −3%)
  • Repeat‑days bonus when the symbol appears on 2+ distinct dates (extra weight for 3–4+ days)
  • Trend bonus when delivery shows a rising pattern across files

Recommendations are mapped from the final score: Strong Buy ≥ 85, Accumulate ≥ 65, Watch ≥ 45, otherwise Avoid.

2. Top Opportunities

Shows the strongest current ideas: filter to Strong Buy and Accumulate names, then display the top few by score. These are the highest‑conviction accumulation candidates right now.

3. Pre‑Breakout Opportunities

Focuses on early setups that are close to moving:

  • Filter to stocks with Watch recommendation or Breakout candidate signal
  • Exclude symbols already in Top Opportunities to avoid duplication
  • From the remainder, show the top few by score with their pre‑breakout action

This gives a watchlist of constructive names that may become future Top Opportunities if follow‑through improves.
