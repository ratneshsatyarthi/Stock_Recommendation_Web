Signal rules

1. Global scoring

Each symbol gets a composite score from delivery, trade, price change, repeat days and trend:

  • Delivery vs Trade ratio: ratio = Delv x / (Trade x + 0.1)
  • Delivery spike bonus when Delv x > 1.3 × avg Delv x, else smaller bonus if Delv x > avg
  • Trade spike bonus when Trade x > 1.5 × avg Trade x
  • Price change bonus / penalty from Chg% (strongly positive, mildly positive, or < −3%)
  • Repeat‑days bonus when the symbol appears on 2+ distinct dates (extra weight for 3–4+ days)
  • Trend bonus when delivery shows a rising pattern across files

Recommendations are mapped from the final score: Strong Buy ≥ 85, Accumulate ≥ 65, Watch ≥ 45, otherwise Avoid.
